## SOW Outline

> Template for creating Statement of Work documents

## Structure

### Executive Summary
- Brief overview of the engagement
- Objectives and expected outcomes
- Timeline summary

### Scope of Work
- What's included (detailed list)
- What's explicitly excluded
- Assumptions and dependencies

### Deliverables
- Specific deliverables with acceptance criteria
- Timeline for each deliverable
- Format and documentation requirements

### Success Criteria
- Measurable success criteria
- How success will be validated
- Sign-off process

### Timeline and Milestones
- Project timeline with key milestones
- Dependencies and critical path
- Buffer time and risk factors

### Resources and Responsibilities
- Customer responsibilities
- Vendor responsibilities
- Required resources and access

### Terms and Conditions
- Payment terms
- Change management process
- Intellectual property
- Confidentiality

### Appendices
- Technical requirements
- Architecture diagrams
- Reference documentation
