## Post-Implementation Follow-Up Template

> Use this template for follow-up communication after implementation handoff

**Subject:** Post-Implementation Check-In - [Customer Name]

Hi [Customer Contact Name],

I wanted to check in now that you've had [time period] to operate the solution. I hope everything is going smoothly.

**Quick Check-In:**
- How is the solution performing?
- Are there any issues or questions?
- Is the team comfortable with operations?
- Do you need any additional support?

**Resources Available:**
- Documentation: [Link]
- Runbooks: [Link]
- Support channel: [Contact method]
- Escalation path: [Process]

**Next Steps:**
- [If applicable: scheduled check-in, training, optimization]
- [If applicable: expansion opportunities]
- [If applicable: reference customer discussion]

**Feedback Request:**
Your feedback helps us improve. If you have a few minutes, I'd love to hear:
- What's working well?
- What could be improved?
- Any suggestions for future enhancements?

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything. I'm here to help ensure your success.

Best regards,  
[Your Name]

---

**Variations:**

**1-Week Follow-Up:**
"Now that you've had a week to operate the solution, I wanted to check in. How is everything going? Are there any questions or issues we should address?"

**30-Day Follow-Up:**
"It's been 30 days since handoff. I'd love to hear how things are going. Are you seeing the expected value? Any challenges we should address?"

**If Issues Arise:**
"I heard you're experiencing [issue]. Let's schedule a call to troubleshoot. I'm available [times]. In the meantime, [suggested action]."

**If Everything is Going Well:**
"Great to hear everything is working well! I'd love to discuss [expansion opportunity/reference customer/optimization] when you have time."

**Transition to Success Team:**
"As we discussed, [Success Team Member] will be taking over as your primary contact. They'll reach out to schedule an introduction call. I'll be available for transition questions over the next [time period]."
