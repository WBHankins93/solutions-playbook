**Variations:**

**For Technical Issues:**
"We've identified the root cause: [technical issue]. Our engineering team is working on a fix. Expected resolution: [Date]. In the meantime, [workaround if available]."

**For Timeline Concerns:**
"I understand the timeline is important. We're [action] to get back on track. Revised timeline: [dates]. If this doesn't work, let's discuss alternatives."

**For Relationship Issues:**
"I want to make sure we're aligned and working well together. I've brought in [Name, Title] to help reset and ensure we're meeting your expectations going forward."

**For Scope Disputes:**
"I understand you'd like [request]. That's not in our current scope, which we agreed was focused on [original scope]. We can definitely add [request] to [post-POC roadmap/follow-up phase]. For now, let's focus on [original scope] so we can move forward."

**For Critical Escalations:**
"I understand the urgency and impact. I've escalated this to [Name, Title] who will be directly involved. We're treating this as a priority and will provide updates [frequency]. Let's schedule a call [time] to discuss the path forward."
