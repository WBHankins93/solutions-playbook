## Core workflows

### Engagements

- [Discovery](pre-sales/discovery.md) - Run discovery calls that produce clear requirements, risks, and next steps.
- [Technical Deep Dive](pre-sales/technical-deep-dive.md) - Facilitate architecture review and technical validation conversations.
- [Demo](pre-sales/demo.md) - Prepare and deliver demos tied to customer outcomes.

- [POC Execution](pre-sales/poc-execution.md) - Manage proof work, checkpoints, risks, and closeout.

### Implementation

- [Kickoff](implementation/kickoff.md) - Align customer and internal teams before implementation starts.
- [Mid-Implementation](implementation/mid-implementation.md) - Inspect progress, blockers, risk, and scope during delivery.
- [Troubleshooting](implementation/troubleshooting.md) - Structure technical blocker investigation and communication.
- [Handoff](implementation/handoff.md) - Transition ownership with decisions, runbooks, risks, and next steps.
