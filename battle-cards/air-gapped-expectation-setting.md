# Air-Gapped Expectation Setting

## Situation

The customer expects a deployment or POC in an air-gapped environment to move at the same speed as an internet-connected environment.

## What the customer may say

"It's air-gapped, but installation should be standard."

## What you should say

"Air-gapped work is doable, but it changes the delivery plan. We need to account for artifact transfer, dependency mirroring, access windows, validation steps, and the customer-owned approval path."

## What not to say

"Air-gapped should not change much."

## Follow-up question

"What is the approved process for moving images, packages, logs, and troubleshooting artifacts into and out of the environment?"
