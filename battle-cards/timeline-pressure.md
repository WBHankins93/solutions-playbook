# Timeline Pressure

## Situation

The customer, seller, or internal team asks for a compressed timeline that risks quality, prerequisites, or decision confidence.

## What the customer may say

"We need this completed by Friday."

## What you should say

"We can work backward from Friday, but we need to be explicit about tradeoffs. Let's confirm the decision-critical items, prerequisites, owners, and what we will defer if time gets tight."

## What not to say

"No problem, we can make that happen."

## Follow-up question

"What decision or business event is driving that date?"
