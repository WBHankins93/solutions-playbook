# POC Success Criteria

## Situation

The POC is starting, but the customer has vague goals, unclear acceptance criteria, or multiple stakeholders defining success differently.

## What the customer may say

"We'll know it works when we see it."

## What you should say

"Let's make success observable before we begin. We should define the required use cases, pass/fail criteria, data needed, owners, and decision date so everyone evaluates the same outcome."

## What not to say

"We'll figure out success criteria as we go."

## Follow-up question

"What must be true at the end of the POC for you to recommend moving forward?"
