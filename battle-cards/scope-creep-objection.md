# Scope Creep Objection

## Situation

The customer asks to add new users, integrations, use cases, environments, or technical validation after scope has already been agreed.

## What the customer may say

"Can we just add one more workflow before we call the POC complete?"

## What you should say

"We can evaluate that. To protect the timeline, let's separate agreed success criteria from new scope. If this new item is required for a decision, we should update the plan, owner, and date together."

## What not to say

"Sure, that should be easy."

## Follow-up question

"Is this new request a blocker to the buying decision, or a next-phase requirement?"
