**Understand the Context**

- What's the deal status? (Prospect, POC, signed?)
- What product/solution are they evaluating?
- What's the revenue potential? Timeline pressure?
- Any politics or sensitivities to be aware of?
- Who else is involved internally? (Sales, other SEs, leadership)

- Check CRM for notes, call recordings, emails
- Look for any technical documentation or architecture notes
- Review past status updates or trip reports
- Check if there's a handoff document from another SE
- Look for any environment-specific notes or constraints

   - Note any constraints or special requirements
