## Requesting Help from Engineering

### Context

You're stuck and need help from engineering. Make it easy for them to help you.

### Before You Ask

- [ ] Have you actually tried to solve it yourself?
- [ ] Can you reproduce the issue?
- [ ] Do you have logs, error messages, or screenshots?
- [ ] Have you checked documentation or past tickets?

### How to Ask

Hey [Name/Team],

I'm working on [customer/engagement] and hit an issue I could use help with.

**What I'm trying to do:**

[One sentence]

**What's happening:**

[Error message, behavior, etc.]

**What I've tried:**

- Tried X, result was Y
- Checked Z, didn't help

**Environment details:**

- Kubernetes version: X
- Helm chart version: Y
- [Other relevant details]

**Urgency:**

[Customer waiting / blocking demo tomorrow / can wait a few days]

Happy to jump on a call or share screen if helpful.

### Why This Works

Shows you've done homework. Respects their time. Gets you help faster.
