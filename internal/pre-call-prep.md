   - Who's leading the call?
   - Who's taking notes?
   - Who handles what questions?

4. **Desired outcome**
   - What do we want to walk away with?
   - What's the next step we're aiming for?

5. **Landmines**
   - Features we don't have that might come up?
   - Competitor mentions?
   - Pricing concerns?
